Eat That Frog! - Day 1

Intro

The need to be selective
  • Get one thing done quickly and well
  • Set clear priorities
  • Get important things done quickly
The truth about frogs
  • If you have 2 frogs, eat the uglier one first
  • Resist the temptation to start with the easiest tasks
  • Don't sit and look at your frog
Take action immediately
  • action orientation helps people stand out and get promoted
  • failure to execute is one of the biggest problems
Develop the habits of success
  • Success is determined by habits
  • Create a habit of starting and completing important tasks
Develop a positive addiction
  • become addicted to success and contribution
3 Ds
  • Decision to develop a habit
  • Discipline in completing important tasks
  • Determination until habit is locked in

Visualize yourself as you want to be. Think continuously about rewards and results.

Intro

Clarity is most important in personal productivity

Procrastination is caused by vagueness about what is to be done, in what order, and why

Tip: think on paper

Steps to success

  1. Decide what you want
  2. Write it down. It is a wish until you write it down
  3. Set a deadline and subdeadlines
  4. make a list of everything you have to do to achieve it, maintain the list
  5. organize the list into a plan. Prioritize and sequence it
  6. Take action on your plan immediately. An average executed plan is better than a perfect plan where nothing is done
  7. Do Something every day that moves towards goal
Review goals daily
